Central Midori International

Founded in Singapore in 1988 with the transfer of technology from Japan, CMI is recognized in South East Asia for his expertise in flexible printed electronic. Our main products include membrane switch, graphic overlay, sensor and security foil. We have enjoyed a steady growth by expanding our position into electronic market segments like Industrial Control, Instrumentation, Banking and Medical. Our mission is to enable our customers to make their products better, cheaper and faster through our core technology expertise, process efficiency and a nimble organization. We focus on customers who require continuous assistance from design through manufacturing. CMI is a subsidiary of Demmel Gruppe with locations in Germany, Switzerland and China.
