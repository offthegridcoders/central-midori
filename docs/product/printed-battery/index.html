Details

Central Midori is partnering with Enfucell to design, manufacture and sell printed batteries to its customers.

Enfucell SoftBattery® is a disposable, thin and flexible printed power source for devices like wireless sensors for healthcare, sports and logistics. Batteries are custom-designed with the client, taking the specific purpose of each application into account..

CMI is working closely with Enfucell to offer application engineering services and help customers taking full advantage of the broad potential of the thin printed battery technology.

The chemistry is based on zinc and manganese dioxide, and zinc chloride as an electrolyte.

SoftBattery® is available in 1.5V and 3.0V. Size can vary from 1 cm² to 100 cm² and in various shapes and forms.Typical battery thickness is 0.6mm.
