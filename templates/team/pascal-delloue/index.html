Pascal Delloue

Pascal Delloue joined Central Midori in 2014 as General Manager to lead a new strategy towards printed electronics. He brings more than 30 years of experience in electronic manufacturing, with extensive traveling in North America, Europe, and Asia.

Originally from France, he moved to New England in 1998 and is now an American citizen. During his free time, Pascal enjoys exploring Southeast Asia with his wife and long-distance paddling between the small islands south of Singapore.
