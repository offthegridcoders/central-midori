Serene Chan joined Central Midori in 2003. She’s responsible for the company’s finance, accounting, administration and human resources department. She brings over 20 years of experience in finance and accounting sectors.

Prior to joining CMI, Serene served in various accounting positions. She holds a diploma in accounting from the London of Chamber Commerce and Industry. During her free time, she enjoys yoga, traveling and spending time with her family.
