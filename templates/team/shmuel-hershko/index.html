Shmuel Hershko

Shmuel Hershko (Sam) joined CMI in 2015 as plant manager. In the last 15 years, he has successfully led complex projects in an international environment throughout Europe, Israel, and Asia. Over the years he’s showed extensive expertise in management and process development.

After finishing his military service in the Israeli Special Forces, Shmuel obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Engineering from Ariel University. He’s an avid outrigger canoe paddler and enjoys any activity that can be done in the great outdoors. Together with his wife Ricki, Shmuel is raising two daughters and a son.
